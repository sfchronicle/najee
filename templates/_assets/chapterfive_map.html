Tracking Najee Harris' recruitment

Antioch High's Najee Harris, the nation's consensus No. 1 recruit, has fielded scholarship offers from nearly every major college football program in the nation. He verbally committed to Alabama as a high school sophomore in April 2015, but he has kept his recruitment open and made unofficial visits to several top schools this past summer. Harris can't officially commit until national signing day on Feb. 1.
